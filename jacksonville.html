Why Jacksonville

Why Jacksonville Is the Place to Build

Northeast Florida is booming — and Jacksonville's combination of space, affordability, and coastal living makes it one of the best places in the state to build a custom home from the ground up.

📈

Largest City, Biggest Opportunity

Jacksonville covers over 875 square miles, making it the largest city by land area in the continental U.S. That means more available lots at more affordable prices than nearly any major Florida metro. Massive growth is fueling demand across Duval and St. Johns Counties.

🏖️

Beaches & River Living

From the Atlantic Ocean shoreline to the winding St. Johns River, Jacksonville offers an outdoor lifestyle unlike anywhere else in Florida. No state income tax, world-class fishing, surfing, and miles of waterfront — all at a fraction of South Florida prices.

🏡

Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Jacksonville?

Everything Northeast Florida homebuilders need to know before breaking ground.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Jacksonville? +
Custom home construction in Jacksonville typically ranges from $150 to $350+ per square foot depending on your build level and finishes. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $700,000. BuilderK offers three build tiers: Standard ($150–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). Coastal builds near the beaches may cost more due to wind mitigation and flood zone requirements. Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Jacksonville? +
A typical custom home in Jacksonville takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. The full timeline including design, financing, and permitting is usually 9 to 14 months from your first consultation. Builds in beach communities or flood zones may require additional permitting time of 2 to 4 weeks. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Duval County or St. Johns County? +
Yes. If you own a lot in Duval County, St. Johns County, or Clay County, BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Jacksonville's large lot sizes are ideal for custom construction. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
What permits are required to build a home in Jacksonville? +
All new home construction in Jacksonville requires building permits from Duval County or the City of Jacksonville. This includes site plans, engineering, and inspections. Coastal properties near the beaches also need flood zone compliance and wind load certifications. BuilderK handles the entire permitting process so you don't have to navigate any of it yourself.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan in Jacksonville? +
Most construction lenders in the Jacksonville area require a minimum credit score of 680 for a one-time close construction loan. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What neighborhoods in Jacksonville does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout the greater Jacksonville metro including Mandarin, San Marco, Riverside, Avondale, Jacksonville Beach, Atlantic Beach, Neptune Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, Nocatee, Fleming Island, and Orange Park. If you have a lot anywhere in Duval, St. Johns, or Clay County, we can build on it.
