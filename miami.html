Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Miami?

Everything Miami homebuilders need to know before breaking ground in South Florida.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Miami? +
Custom home construction in Miami typically ranges from $180 to $450+ per square foot depending on your build level, finishes, and lot requirements. A 2,500 sq ft home generally costs between $450,000 and $1,125,000+. BuilderK offers three build tiers: Standard ($180–$220/sqft), Mid-Range ($220–$300/sqft), and Luxury ($300+/sqft). Miami's stricter hurricane codes and NOA material requirements contribute to higher baseline costs than other Florida markets. Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Miami? +
A typical custom home in Miami takes 8 to 14 months from permit approval to move-in. Miami-Dade County permitting often requires additional time for NOA product reviews and enhanced wind load compliance. The full timeline including design, financing, and permitting is usually 12 to 18 months from your first consultation. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Miami-Dade County? +
Yes. If you own a lot anywhere in Miami-Dade County — from Coral Gables and Pinecrest to Homestead and Key Biscayne — BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
What permits and NOA requirements apply to new construction in Miami? +
All new home construction in Miami-Dade County requires building permits with strict wind load compliance. Every exterior product — windows, doors, roofing, and impact-rated materials — must carry a Miami-Dade Notice of Acceptance (NOA) to certify it meets the county's enhanced hurricane standards. BuilderK handles all permitting, NOA documentation, flood zone compliance, and inspection scheduling so you never have to deal with the bureaucracy.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan in Miami? +
Most construction lenders in South Florida require a minimum credit score of 680 for a one-time close construction loan. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What Miami neighborhoods does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout Miami-Dade County including Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, Pinecrest, South Miami, Key Biscayne, Doral, Miami Beach, Palmetto Bay, Kendall, Homestead, and Cutler Bay. Whether you are looking at a waterfront estate in Key Biscayne or an acre lot in Pinecrest, if you have land in Miami-Dade County, we can build on it.
