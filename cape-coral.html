Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Cape Coral?

Everything you need to know before building in Southwest Florida's waterfront capital.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Cape Coral? +
Custom home construction in Cape Coral typically ranges from $160 to $350+ per square foot depending on your build level and whether the lot is waterfront. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $700,000. Canal-front and Gulf-access lots may require additional site work such as seawalls. BuilderK offers three tiers: Standard ($160–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). Pricing reflects base construction for a build-ready lot; government permit and impact fees are billed at cost, and site work beyond standard slab-on-grade construction (land clearing, fill, stem wall or elevated foundations) is quoted separately. Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Cape Coral? +
A typical custom home in Cape Coral takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. The full timeline including design, financing, and Lee County permitting is usually 9 to 14 months. Waterfront builds may require additional time for seawall inspection and flood zone compliance. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Lee County? +
Yes. If you own a lot in Cape Coral, Lee County, or anywhere in Southwest Florida, BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. We build on canal lots, Gulf-access lots, freshwater lots, and dry lots. Learn about financing →
What permits do I need to build a home in Cape Coral? +
All new home construction in Cape Coral requires building permits from both Lee County and the City of Cape Coral. This includes site plans, engineering, flood zone compliance, wind load calculations, and environmental reviews. Canal-front lots may need additional seawall and dock permits. BuilderK handles the entire permitting process so you don't have to navigate it alone.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan? +
Construction loan requirements vary by lender and program. Many programs look for a credit score around 680, while others accept lower scores or are asset-based and weigh the project more than the borrower's credit. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans and handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What areas in Cape Coral does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout Cape Coral and Southwest Florida including SW Cape, NW Cape, SE Cape, Pelican, Burnt Store, Pine Island, Matlacha, Sanibel, Fort Myers Beach, Lehigh Acres, and Bonita Springs. If you have a lot anywhere in Lee County, we can build on it.
