Your Home, Your Choices

Interior Selections

Every finish in your BuilderK home is chosen by you. From cabinets to countertops to the paint on the walls, we walk you through every decision so your home feels like yours from day one.

Fully Customizable at Every Build Level

Unlike other builders who lock you into pre-selected packages, BuilderK lets you choose your own cabinets, countertops, flooring, fixtures, lighting, and paint at every build level. Your structure defines the tier. Your interior is always your call.

Kitchen Cabinets

The kitchen is the center of your home. We offer a full range of cabinet styles, door profiles, materials, and hardware so you can create the exact look you want — from clean modern flat panels to timeless shaker designs.

Shaker Style

The most popular door style in new construction. Clean recessed panels that work with any direction — farmhouse, transitional, or modern.

TimelessVersatileEasy to clean

Flat Panel (Slab)

Smooth, frameless doors for a sleek minimalist look. The go-to for modern and contemporary kitchens. No grooves, no detail — just clean surfaces.

ModernMinimalistSleek

Raised Panel

A center panel raised above the frame for a classic, elegant feel. Perfect for traditional and Mediterranean-inspired Florida homes.

Countertops

Your countertops set the tone for kitchens and bathrooms. We offer everything from engineered quartz to natural stone — each with different durability, maintenance, and aesthetic profiles perfectly suited for Florida living.

Quartz (Engineered Stone)

Non-porous, stain-resistant, and virtually maintenance-free. Consistent patterns throughout the slab. Handles Florida's humidity perfectly — no sealing required, ever.

Non-porousStain resistantNo sealingHumidity proof

Granite (Natural Stone)

Every slab is unique with natural veining and depth. Extremely heat-resistant — set a hot pan right on it. Hundreds of colors from subtle to dramatic. Needs annual sealing.

One-of-a-kindHeat resistantNatural beauty

Quartzite (Natural)

Looks like marble but performs like granite. Incredible veining with the hardness to handle daily kitchen use. The choice for those who want natural beauty without the softness of marble.

Marble lookGranite strengthStunning veining

Marble

Timeless elegance with dramatic veining. Softer than granite — it develops a beautiful patina over time. Best for bathrooms, feature islands, and lovers of lived-in luxury.

TimelessDramatic veiningLuxury feel

Flooring

Florida homes need flooring that handles humidity, heavy foot traffic, and still looks amazing years later. We help you pick the right material for every room — from living areas to wet spaces.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

The #1 choice for Florida new construction. 100% waterproof, scratch-resistant, and available in incredibly realistic wood and stone looks. Comfortable underfoot and easy to maintain.

100% waterproofScratch resistantRealistic texturesFlorida #1

Porcelain Tile

Large-format porcelain (24x24 or 12x48 plank) is the classic Florida flooring. Extremely durable, water-resistant, available in wood-look, marble-look, and concrete finishes.

Extremely durableWater resistantLarge formats

Ceramic Tile

Versatile and reliable for bathrooms, laundry, and wet areas. Endless patterns, colors, and sizes including subway, hexagon, and decorative mosaics.

VersatileWet area idealMany patterns

Engineered Hardwood

Real wood veneer over a stable plywood core. More moisture-resistant than solid hardwood. Beautiful natural warmth for bedrooms and living areas in climate-controlled spaces.

Real woodNatural warmthStable in humidity

Natural Stone (Travertine)

Imported travertine or marble tile for a truly unique floor. Warm tones and Old World character — especially popular in Florida for its Mediterranean feel.

Unique characterWarm tonesPremium
