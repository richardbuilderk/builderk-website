Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

How Builders Knowledge Development Inc. (BuilderK) collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Company Information

Builders Knowledge Development Inc. ("BuilderK," "we," "us," or "our") is a licensed Florida general contractor (CGC License #1537163) headquartered at 4700 Millenia Blvd, Ste 500, Orlando, FL 32839. You can contact us at info@builderk.com or (239) 230-4868.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact or quote request form (name, email, phone, zip code, lot ownership status, budget, timeline, and project details)
  • Contact us via email, phone, or social media
  • Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications
  • Interact with our advertisements on platforms like Meta (Facebook/Instagram) or Google

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Referring website or advertisement

We use Google Analytics (ID: G-S03X1HTEDN) and Google Ads conversion tracking to analyze website traffic and measure advertising performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide project estimates
  • Connect you with construction loan lenders and other partners relevant to your home building project
  • Send you marketing communications about our services (with your consent)
  • Improve our website, services, and advertising effectiveness
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate our website, process forms, and deliver services (e.g., Formspree for form submissions, Vercel for website hosting)
  • Lending partners: When you request financing assistance, we may share your contact information with our lending partners to facilitate your construction loan application
  • Advertising platforms: We use Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and Google advertising platforms which may collect data through pixels and tracking tags on our website
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies from:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how visitors use our site
  • Google Ads: To track conversions from our advertising campaigns
  • Meta Pixel: To measure and optimize our Facebook and Instagram advertising

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Object to the processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@builderk.com.

8. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing info@builderk.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email. Please note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., active contractual obligations).

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (lenders, social media platforms, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
