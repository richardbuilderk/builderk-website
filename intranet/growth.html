Sales playbook

How a prospect becomes a signed custom home — a consistent path so nothing leaks out of the funnel.

01

Lead

Inbound from the campaign, referral or web.

02

Discovery

Qualify lot, budget, timeline & scope.

03

Proposal

Scope, allowances, price & schedule.

04

Contract

Signed agreement → deposit → pre-construction.

  • Discovery — confirm the lot, a realistic budget, the timeline and the scope (custom vs. plan, coastal, etc.).
  • Proposal — clear scope, allowances and a price tied to the estimate; set expectations with the NAHB quality standard.
  • Follow-up cadence — every lead worked on a defined rhythm; nothing goes cold.
  • Pipeline stages — tracked in the CRM so we know conversion and where deals stall.
  • Handoff — a signed contract kicks off the Pre-Construction Checklist.
