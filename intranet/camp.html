Win those six lanes per metro and in 6–12 months BuilderK out-converts the #1 in its zones — that's the road to being one of the great home builders of Florida.

The phone desk — no lost calls (Sofía + 2)

System spec (GHL phone)
  • One tracked number → ring-all / round-robin to the 3 agents during business hours (9am–8pm local, FTSA-safe).
  • Missed call = instant text-back ("It's Sofía with BuilderK — sorry we missed you, what's the best time?") + callback task to the desk. No call ever dies in voicemail silence.
  • After hours: auto-SMS + voicemail transcribed into the contact + first-morning-slot call task.
  • Every call logged on the GHL contact; daily missed-call report to Sofía; weekly answer-rate on the Friday scorecard.

Six content pillars
  • Speed & process — "Empty lot to home in 6–10 months. This is month 2." Episodic per real project.
  • Financing myth-busting — "If you own your lot, your land can be the down payment." (+ qualified-buyer disclaimer)
  • Price transparency — "What $180/sqft actually gets you in Orlando" → calculator CTA. Nobody in the top-10 does this.
  • Real jobsite, no filter — slab pours, trusses, impact windows; Kevin explains one technical decision per video.
  • Your lot — "Your lot in {{city}} has been sitting for years. Here's what it could look like in 8 months."
  • Named case studies — house, city, investment range, real timeline (signed permission first). Our digital model home.
Design studio — Valentina + Flor
  • Valentina (architecture) designs the new homes & visual content that upgrade builderk.com's design section.
  • Flor (interiors/decoration) styles them — finishes, palettes, staging — so every design sells a lifestyle, not a floor plan.
  • They pull city / public record plans of solid, permitted homes as inspiration for a BuilderK model line.
  • Output: 2 new designs/week → renders + carousels + "what $X gets you" assets for Rocío + the website design gallery.
AI production line — Richard + Kevin

Richard & Kevin select and wire the APIs/AI components that multiply output: AI video cutting/captioning for the 95-video archive and new jobsite footage, AI render/staging assists for the design studio, copy assistants tuned to the voice rules, and auto-publishing where safe. Goal: the team curates; the machine produces.

Voice rules (every post).  No AI-tells, contractions on, read it out loud before posting, describe the house and the terms — never the imagined buyer (Fair Housing). Claims like "Instant Equity 20–30%", "0% Down Possible", "150+ units delivered" go through Kevin/Richard compliance review before use (Reg N: substantiate or rephrase as team experience).

Review engine (starts Week 1, runs forever)

List the last 20–30 delivered clients with a live relationship — Debbie at closing is candidate #1. Personal ask from Kevin/Sofía + direct GBP link. Then automatic: Closed Won → N6 review sequence. Target: 15–25 fresh Google reviews in 60 days — that alone beats the #1's stale 13–17 per metro.
