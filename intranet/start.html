Who we are

Company & mission

BuilderK (Builders Knowledge Development Inc.) is a licensed Florida custom home builder — CGC #1537163, based in Orlando. We build custom homes for our own clients and execute the projects developed with our partner BuildShare.ai, to one disciplined standard of safety, quality and on-time delivery.

The rules we abide by

Employee Handbook & SOPs Framework — docs attach here

The handbook is the set of rules and expectations every team member agrees to. New hires read and acknowledge it during onboarding. The full policy document attaches here once finalized; the sections it covers:

Employee Handbook

  • Code of conduct & professionalism
  • Attendance, hours & timekeeping
  • Safety — the jobsite program (see Safety & OSHA)
  • Communications & IT — email, devices, data, confidentiality
  • Equal opportunity & anti-harassment
  • Drug-free workplace
  • PTO, leave & benefits
  • Vehicle / equipment use & expenses

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

The "how we do it here" index — the repeatable procedures behind the work:

  • Set up a new project in BuilderK
  • Run the Pre-Construction Checklist (see the checklist)
  • Issue a PO / award a subcontract
  • Onboard a subcontractor (W-9, COI, MSA)
  • Schedule & record an inspection
  • Submit a lender draw
  • Run the weekly work plan & meeting
  • Close out a project & start the warranty
📎 To finalize
Attach the signed Employee Handbook PDF and link each SOP as it's written. New hires acknowledge the handbook in their offer/HR record.
