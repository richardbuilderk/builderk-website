Pre-Construction Checklist

The 83 tasks that must be handled before Day-1 of any residential project. Contracts signed, deliverables sealed, permits filed, subs contracted, long-lead POs issued, site set up, finance & schedule loaded — then we move to construction.

🎯 Readiness rule
A project moves from pre-construction to IN PROGRESS only when ≥ 80% of these tasks are DONE. The Compliance tasks (section 6) apply to every awarded subcontractor and must be 100% complete before that sub's first payment. Adapt the list to the scope — custom vs spec, coastal vs inland, demo vs vacant lot.
