The platform

What BuildShare.ai is

BuildShare.ai is a real-estate development platform that covers the full development cycle — land acquisition and entitlement, capital formation, construction, delivery and asset management — coordinated by an AI multi-agent engine. It brings three things to the table that turn into work for BuilderK:

  • Land — sourcing, acquisition and entitlement of buildable lots and parcels.
  • Capital — the financial structure that funds each project.
  • Pipeline — a steady flow of projects ready to design and build.

BuilderK is the construction side of that platform — the licensed general contractor that executes the build and delivers the finished home.

One bar

Shared standards

Every project we build with BuildShare runs on the same playbook — the one in this intranet. That's how quality stays consistent no matter the deal or the metro.

  • One Pre-Construction Checklist — the same 83-task, 9-domain standard on every project.
  • One set of codes — see Standards & codes (US, Florida, hurricane & coastal).
  • One safety program — see Safety & OSHA.
  • One quality bar — NAHB performance guidelines, the same QC and closeout on every home.
