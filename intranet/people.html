Our trades

Subcontractors

Subs are how we build. Every subcontractor is onboarded and compliant before their first payment — no exceptions.

Onboarding a sub (before first payment)

  • W-9 on file
  • COI — General Liability (BuilderK as certificate holder)
  • COI — Workers' Comp (or a valid exemption)
  • Master Subcontract Agreement (MSA) signed — plus scope-specific terms
  • Site safety orientation for the crew (see Safety & OSHA)
📄 One MSA, many projects
The Master Subcontract Agreement is signed once and governs all of a sub's work; individual work orders issue under it per project. Compliance is the gate in the Pre-Construction Checklist (section 6).
