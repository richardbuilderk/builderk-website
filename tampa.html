Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Tampa?

Answers to the most common questions Tampa Bay homebuilders ask before breaking ground.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Tampa? +
Custom home construction in Tampa and Hillsborough County typically ranges from $150 to $350+ per square foot depending on your build level, lot location, and finishes. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $700,000. BuilderK offers three build tiers: Standard ($150–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Tampa? +
A typical custom home in Tampa takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. The full timeline including design, financing, and Hillsborough County permitting is usually 9 to 14 months from your first consultation. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Tampa? +
Yes. If you own a lot in Tampa, Hillsborough County, Pinellas County, or Pasco County, BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
Do I need a permit to build a home in Tampa? +
Yes, all new home construction in Tampa requires building permits from Hillsborough County or your local municipality. This includes site plans, engineering, flood zone compliance, wind load calculations, and HOA reviews. BuilderK handles the entire permitting process so you never have to navigate county offices yourself.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan in Tampa? +
Most construction lenders in the Tampa Bay area require a minimum credit score of 680 for a one-time close construction loan. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What neighborhoods in Tampa does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout the greater Tampa Bay metro including South Tampa, Hyde Park, Westchase, Wesley Chapel, Brandon, Riverview, Land O' Lakes, Lutz, New Tampa, Carrollwood, and Seminole Heights. If you have a lot anywhere in Hillsborough, Pinellas, or Pasco County, we can build on it.
