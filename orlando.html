Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Orlando?

Everything Orlando homebuilders need to know before breaking ground.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Orlando? +
Custom home construction in Orlando typically ranges from $160 to $350+ per square foot depending on your build level and finishes. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $700,000. BuilderK offers three build tiers: Standard ($160–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). Pricing reflects base construction for a build-ready lot; government permit and impact fees are billed at cost, and site work beyond standard slab-on-grade construction (land clearing, fill, stem wall or elevated foundations) is quoted separately. Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Orlando? +
A typical custom home in Orlando takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. The full timeline including design, financing, and permitting is usually 9 to 14 months from your first consultation. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Orlando? +
Yes. If you own a lot in Orlando, Orange County, Osceola County, or Seminole County, BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
Do I need a permit to build a home in Orlando? +
Yes, all new home construction in Orlando requires building permits from Orange County or your local municipality. This includes site plans, engineering, flood zone compliance, wind load calculations, and HOA reviews. BuilderK handles the entire permitting process so you don't have to deal with any of it.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan in Orlando? +
Construction loan requirements vary by lender and program. Many programs look for a credit score around 680, while others accept lower scores or are asset-based and weigh the project more than the borrower's credit. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What neighborhoods in Orlando does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout the greater Orlando metro including Winter Park, Lake Nona, Dr. Phillips, Windermere, Celebration, Kissimmee, Winter Garden, Ocoee, Apopka, Clermont, and Davenport. If you have a lot anywhere in Orange, Osceola, or Seminole County, we can build on it.
