Common Questions

Building a Custom Home on the Space Coast?

Everything Brevard County homebuilders need to know before breaking ground.

How much does it cost to build a custom home on the Space Coast? +
Custom home construction in Brevard County typically ranges from $160 to $320+ per square foot depending on your build level, finishes, and proximity to the coast. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $640,000. BuilderK offers three build tiers: Standard ($160–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). Pricing reflects base construction for a build-ready lot; government permit and impact fees are billed at cost, and site work beyond standard slab-on-grade construction (land clearing, fill, stem wall or elevated foundations) is quoted separately. Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Melbourne, FL? +
A typical custom home in Melbourne takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. The full timeline including design, financing, and permitting is usually 9 to 14 months from your first consultation. Coastal builds may require additional engineering reviews that can add to the permitting phase. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Brevard County? +
Yes. If you own a lot anywhere in Brevard County — from beachside parcels in Indialantic and Melbourne Beach to master-planned communities like Viera and Baytree — BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
Do I need a permit to build a home in Melbourne or Brevard County? +
Yes, all new home construction in Melbourne and Brevard County requires building permits. Coastal properties have additional requirements including FEMA flood zone compliance, wind load calculations rated for coastal high-wind zones, impact-rated windows and doors, and coastal construction setback reviews. BuilderK handles the entire Brevard County permitting process so you don't have to deal with any of it.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan on the Space Coast? +
Construction loan requirements vary by lender and program. Many programs look for a credit score around 680, while others accept lower scores or are asset-based and weigh the project more than the borrower's credit. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What areas on the Space Coast does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout Brevard County and the Space Coast including Melbourne, Palm Bay, Viera, Cocoa Beach, Rockledge, Merritt Island, Satellite Beach, Indialantic, Melbourne Beach, Titusville, Suntree, and Baytree. If you have a lot anywhere in Brevard County, we can build on it.
