Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Ocala?

Everything you need to know before building in Marion County.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Ocala? +
Ocala is one of the most affordable places to build a custom home in Florida. Construction typically ranges from $150 to $350+ per square foot depending on finishes. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $700,000 — but your total project cost is significantly lower than coastal areas because Marion County land is a fraction of the price. BuilderK offers three tiers: Standard ($150–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Ocala? +
A typical custom home in Ocala takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. Marion County permitting tends to move faster than coastal counties, which can shave weeks off your timeline. The full process including design, financing, and permitting is usually 8 to 13 months from your first consultation. BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout the entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Marion County? +
Absolutely. Marion County is known for large, affordable parcels — many between 1 and 5+ acres. If you own land anywhere in Marion County — Ocala, Belleview, Dunnellon, Silver Springs, Summerfield, or beyond — BuilderK can build on it. Your land equity counts as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
What about permits, well, and septic for rural Ocala lots? +
Many Marion County lots sit outside city water and sewer service areas, requiring a private well and septic system. BuilderK handles everything — well permits through the water management district, septic design and permitting through the Marion County Health Department, plus all standard building permits. Rural permitting in Marion County is generally simpler and faster than coastal jurisdictions.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan in Ocala? +
Most construction lenders require a minimum credit score of 680 for a one-time close construction loan. Because Ocala land prices are lower, your total loan amount is often smaller than coastal builds — which can make approval easier. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork. Check your eligibility →
What areas in and around Ocala does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout Marion County including Ocala, SE Ocala, NW Ocala, Silver Springs Shores, Belleview, Dunnellon, On Top of the World, Stone Creek, Golden Hills, Summerfield, Anthony, and Marion Oaks. If you have a lot anywhere in Marion County, we can build on it.
