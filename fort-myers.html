Common Questions

Building a Custom Home in Fort Myers?

Everything Southwest Florida homebuilders need to know before breaking ground.

How much does it cost to build a custom home in Fort Myers? +
Custom home construction in Fort Myers typically ranges from $150 to $350+ per square foot depending on finishes and hurricane-rated specifications. A 2,000 sq ft home generally costs between $300,000 and $700,000. BuilderK offers three tiers: Standard ($150–$180/sqft), Mid-Range ($180–$220/sqft), and Luxury ($220+/sqft). All tiers include current Florida Building Code 8th Edition compliance. Use our free calculator for a personalized estimate.
How long does it take to build a custom home in Fort Myers? +
A typical custom home in Fort Myers takes 6 to 10 months from permit approval to move-in. The full timeline including design, financing, and Lee County permitting is usually 9 to 14 months from your first consultation. Post-Hurricane Ian permitting timelines have stabilized, and BuilderK provides real-time progress updates throughout your entire build.
Can I build on my own lot in Lee County? +
Yes. If you own a lot in Fort Myers, Lehigh Acres, Gateway, Estero, or anywhere in Lee County, BuilderK can build your custom home on it. Your land equity can count as your down payment, potentially allowing 0% additional cash down on a construction loan. Learn about financing →
What permits and building codes apply after Hurricane Ian? +
All new construction in Lee County must comply with the Florida Building Code 8th Edition, which includes updated wind load requirements, impact-rated windows and doors, reinforced roof-to-wall connections, and flood zone elevation standards. BuilderK handles the entire permitting process — including engineering, flood zone compliance, and all post-Ian code requirements — so you never have to visit a county office.
What credit score do I need for a construction loan in Fort Myers? +
Most construction lenders in Southwest Florida require a minimum credit score of 680 for a one-time close construction loan. BuilderK connects you with lenders who specialize in construction-to-permanent loans, and we handle all the builder paperwork to streamline your approval. Check your eligibility →
What neighborhoods in Fort Myers does BuilderK serve? +
We build throughout the greater Fort Myers area including South Fort Myers, Gateway, Iona, San Carlos Park, Lehigh Acres, Estero, Bonita Springs, Fort Myers Beach, Buckingham, Alva, and the Daniels Corridor. If you have a lot anywhere in Lee County, we can build on it.
